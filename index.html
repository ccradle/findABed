What This Does

An outreach worker searches for available beds on their phone. They see what's open right now, hold a bed in three taps, and the shelter coordinator knows they're coming. The platform protects domestic violence shelter locations through zero-PII referral design — no client name, no address in the system, ever. It's free, open-source, and self-hosted — your community owns its data.
